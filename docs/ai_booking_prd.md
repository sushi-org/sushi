
# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Product: AI Booking Automation Assistant for Appointment-Based Businesses

---

# 1. Overview of Product

An AI-powered booking automation assistant designed for appointment-based businesses (e.g., beauty clinics, dental clinics, salons, wellness centers) that automatically handles customer inquiries, answers FAQs, and converts chats into confirmed bookings across WhatsApp, Instagram, and web chat.

The product acts as a 24/7 AI receptionist that reduces manual front-desk workload while increasing booking conversions and customer retention.

Core Value Proposition:
"Customer agent that converts chats into bookings and repeat customers for appointment-based businesses."

---

# 2. Target Market

### Primary Target (Initial Beachhead)
- Beauty clinics
- Aesthetic clinics
- Dental clinics
- Salons & spas
- Wellness & facial studios
- Tuition & enrichment centers

### Customer Profile
- Small to mid-sized SMBs (1–10 staff)
- High volume of WhatsApp / Instagram inquiries
- Manual booking via chat or phone
- No dedicated front desk automation tools
- Located in urban markets (e.g., Singapore, SEA)

### Secondary Expansion Market
- Multi-location clinic chains
- Fitness studios
- Medical practices
- Service-based appointment businesses

---

# 3. Problems We Are Solving

### 1. Manual Chat Overload
Front desk staff spend significant time replying to repetitive inquiries:
- Pricing questions
- Service availability
- Promotions
- Booking requests

### 2. Missed Leads & Slow Response Time
Delayed responses result in:
- Lost bookings
- Customer drop-off
- Poor customer experience

### 3. Fragmented Booking Process
Current workflow is inefficient:
- Chats in WhatsApp/Instagram
- Calendars in Google/Outlook
- No unified system
- High risk of double bookings

### 4. No Automated Follow-Ups
Businesses fail to:
- Recover unbooked inquiries
- Remind customers for repeat visits
- Reactivate inactive customers

### 5. Lack of Visibility into ROI
Owners cannot easily track:
- Inquiry-to-booking conversion
- Revenue from chat channels
- AI vs staff performance

---

# 4. Features (Condensed into 3 Core Pillars)

## 4.1 AI Receptionist (Conversation Automation)
- Unified inbox (WhatsApp, Instagram, Web chat)
- Automated replies to FAQs (pricing, services, policies)
- Intent detection (booking, inquiry, support)
- Smart conversation handling in natural language
- Human escalation for complex cases
- Customizable tone and knowledge base

Outcome: Reduces manual replying workload and provides 24/7 customer response.

---

## 4.2 Automated Booking Engine (Core Scheduling System)
- In-chat appointment booking (no external links)
- Smart slot recommendations based on availability
- Internal calendar (source of truth)
- Google Calendar & Outlook sync (optional)
- Staff and service scheduling management
- Automated confirmations, reminders, rescheduling & cancellations
- Double-booking prevention and booking rules (buffers, durations)

Outcome: Converts conversations directly into confirmed bookings with minimal staff involvement.

---

## 4.3 Growth Automation & Customer Memory (Lightweight CRM)
- Automatic customer profile creation from chats
- Conversation history and visit tracking
- Follow-ups for unbooked inquiries
- Repeat visit reminders (e.g., 4–6 week nudges)
- Reactivation campaigns for inactive customers
- Basic analytics dashboard:
  - Inquiry-to-booking conversion rate
  - AI-handled vs human-handled conversations
  - Bookings and revenue influenced by AI

Outcome: Increases retention, repeat visits, and measurable ROI from automation.

---

## Supporting Capabilities (Platform Foundations)
- Multi-location support (future)
- Role-based staff access
- Business configuration (services, pricing, hours, policies)
- Secure data storage and audit logs
